# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Operations Manager

### Role Summary
Operations Managers ensure the reliability, performance, and scalability of production systems. They manage infrastructure, monitor system health, and coordinate incident response to maintain high availability.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor system health, uptime, and performance metrics
- Manage infrastructure provisioning and capacity planning
- Coordinate incident response and root cause analysis
- Maintain runbooks and operational documentation
- Implement and improve deployment automation and CI/CD pipelines
- Collaborate with developers on observability and reliability

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Minimize incident response and recovery time
- Optimize infrastructure costs and resource utilization
- Improve deployment frequency and success rate

### Typical Communication
- Daily system health reports and incident updates
- Weekly capacity planning and infrastructure reviews
- Incident post-mortems and action items
- Deployment coordination and release notifications

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With PM/PdM**: Align on service level objectives (SLOs) and availability requirements
- **With Developers**: Collaborate on deployment strategies, observability, and performance optimization
- **With Security Lead**: Coordinate on security patches and compliance requirements
- **With Support Lead**: Share incident insights and customer-impacting issues

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create intuitive, user-centered designs that enhance usability and accessibility. They conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and ensure design consistency across the product.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define and maintain design systems and style guides
- Collaborate with product and engineering on feature design
- Ensure accessibility standards are met (WCAG, ADA compliance)
- Validate designs through user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement
- Reduce user friction and support requests
- Maintain design consistency and brand identity
- Ensure accessibility for all users

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with PM and engineering
- User research findings and usability reports
- Design specs and style guide documentation
- Feedback sessions and iteration updates

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With PM/PdM**: Translate product requirements into user-centered designs
- **With Developers**: Provide design specs and collaborate on implementation feasibility
- **With Data Analyst**: Use analytics to inform design decisions and measure success
- **With Support Lead**: Gather user feedback to identify usability issues

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure the security and compliance of systems and data. They identify vulnerabilities, implement security controls, conduct security reviews, and respond to security incidents.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security assessments and threat modeling
- Review code and infrastructure for security vulnerabilities
- Define and enforce security policies and standards
- Manage security incident response and remediation
- Ensure compliance with security frameworks (SOC2, ISO, GDPR)
- Provide security training and guidance to teams

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and attack surface
- Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
- Reduce mean time to detect and respond to security incidents
- Foster security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews and vulnerability reports
- Incident response communications and post-mortems
- Compliance status updates and audit findings
- Security training sessions and best practice guidelines

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With PM/PdM**: Balance security requirements with product timelines
- **With Developers**: Provide security guidance and review code for vulnerabilities
- **With Operations Manager**: Coordinate on infrastructure security and incident response
- **With Data Analyst**: Ensure data privacy and secure analytics practices

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts collect, analyze, and interpret data to inform product decisions and measure success. They create dashboards, generate insights, and help teams understand user behavior and product performance.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Build dashboards and reporting tools
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Conduct A/B testing and experiment analysis
- Provide data-driven insights for product decisions
- Ensure data quality and governance

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Improve visibility into product and business metrics
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth
- Ensure data accuracy and accessibility

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews and trend analysis
- Experiment results and recommendations
- Dashboard demos and data literacy training
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and insights

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With PM/PdM**: Define success metrics and analyze product performance
- **With Developers**: Collaborate on instrumentation and data collection
- **With UX/UI Designer**: Provide usage data to inform design decisions
- **With Support Lead**: Analyze support trends and customer feedback data

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads manage customer support operations and ensure timely, effective resolution of customer issues. They coordinate support teams, maintain knowledge bases, and bridge communication between customers and product/engineering teams.

### Responsibilities
- Manage support ticket queue and escalation workflows
- Maintain support documentation and knowledge bases
- Coordinate customer issue resolution with engineering
- Track support metrics (response time, resolution time, CSAT)
- Identify recurring issues and advocate for product improvements
- Train and mentor support team members

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Minimize response and resolution times
- Reduce support volume through proactive improvements
- Ensure support quality and consistency

### Typical Communication
- Daily support queue reviews and escalations
- Weekly support metrics and trend reports
- Customer feedback summaries and feature requests
- Release notes and customer-facing announcements

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With PM/PdM**: Provide customer feedback and advocate for user needs
- **With Developers**: Coordinate on bug fixes and technical investigations
- **With Operations Manager**: Collaborate on incident response and customer communications
- **With UX/UI Designer**: Share user pain points to inform design improvements
